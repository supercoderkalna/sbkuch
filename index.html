before you ask

Questions partners ask us first.

01 How much does it cost to become a retailer?

Nothing to join. Zero joining fee at the retailer level — you only need the mATM and biometric device, which we deliver to your shop as part of onboarding.

02 How fast can I actually go live?

Digital services (UPI, bill payments, recharge) go live the same day you complete KYC — no wait. Your mATM device and biometric scanner are delivered to your shop within 48 hours, after which your full counter is operational.

03 Is there a contract or lock-in period?

No lock-in. You can stop offering any service at any time. We'd rather earn your business every month than trap you in a contract.

04 What happens if a transaction fails?

Failed transactions are auto-reversed to the customer's account, typically within minutes and almost always same-day. Our 24×7 support team tracks every failure until it's resolved.

05 When do I actually get paid?

Settlements run daily, straight to your bank account. You're never waiting weeks — or holding your own float — for money you've already earned.

06 Is my customers' data safe with SBKuch?

Yes — we're RBI and NPCI compliant, ISO 27001 certified, and all data is stored on Indian infrastructure with 256-bit encryption.
